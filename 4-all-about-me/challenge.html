Challenge: All About Me Webpage

Part One

Make an "All About Me" webpage with HTML. Must include:

  • One header (<h1>, etc)
  • One picture (of you, of things you like, etc)
  • A "Things I Want to Learn" section with an ordered list
  • A "My Favorite Things" section with an unordered list
  • Centered text, somewhere
  • A hyperlink to another web page
  • A "contact me" form that asks for a name, email, and message

Example Solution: All About Ben Centra

Part Two

Style your "All About Me" webpage with CSS. Must include:

  • <div> tags around each section of content, each with a unique "id" element
  • A background color for the whole page
  • Custom fonts - one for headers, one for all other text
  • Your main content, centerd on the page, with a different background color
  • The lists items are converted to <div> tags that are styled as "columns"
  • Bonus: Style the "contact me" form elements

Example Solution: All About Ben Centra
